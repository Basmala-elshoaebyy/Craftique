# Craftique
UXUI Creative App
It’s a platform that connects people who have creative skills with people who want to learn them.
Instructors (like candle makers, accessory designers, and other craft artists) can showcase their work, sell tutorial videos, and promote offline workshops.
Learners can access tutorials, develop their skills, share their work, and even market themselves.
In short, it’s a creative skill marketplace and community that connects makers with learners
