# Craftique
UXUI Creative App  that connects people who have creative skills with people who want to learn them.
Instructors (like candle makers, accessory designers, and other craft artists) can showcase their work, sell tutorial videos.
Learners can access tutorials, develop their skills, share their work, and even market themselves.
In short, it’s a creative skill marketplace and community that connects makers with learners

#  Project Overview

The goal of Craftique is to create a platform that:

- Connects creative instructors with aspiring learners.
- Enables instructors to showcase their handmade products and expertise.
- Allows creators to publish and monetize tutorial videos.
- Helps learners discover new creative skills through structured tutorials.
- Encourages users to share their own work and become part of a creative community.
- Supports creators in growing their audience and personal brand.


---

#  Target Users

###  Instructors

Creative professionals such as:

- Candle Makers
- Accessory Designers
- Crochet Artists
- Resin Artists
- Pottery Makers
- Handmade Product Creators

who want to:

- Showcase their work
- Sell tutorials
- Build their audience
- Generate income

---

###  Learners

People interested in learning creative skills who want to:

- Discover new crafts
- Watch tutorials
- Practice their skills
- Share their creations
- Connect with instructors
